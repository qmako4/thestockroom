Is this for beginners?
+
Yes — loads of our members started with zero sales. The community is genuinely friendly about beginner questions, and the Playbook walks you through setup from scratch.
What if I'm just starting out?
+
Perfect timing honestly. Starting with a clear framework beats flailing for six months then giving up. We've got a "first 30 days" track specifically for new sellers.
Do I need to be on Depop already?
+
Nope. Plenty of members sell on Vinted, eBay, or a mix. The core principles (sourcing, branding, funneling traffic) apply across platforms.
